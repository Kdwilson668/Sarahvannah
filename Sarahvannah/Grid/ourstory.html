Our Story

We started making our soaps and lotions for ourselves as part of our goals to be more self-sufficient. We gave them away to friends and family and they loved them and asked for more, Saravannah Farms was born.

We wanted to get back to the basics when we started making our natural soaps and lotions. Free from concerning artificial ingredients and fragrances found in common products. We wanted to share the natural wonders of goat milk skin care products with everyone and be able to sell them to them at a price everyone could afford.
